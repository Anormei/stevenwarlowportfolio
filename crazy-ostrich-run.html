Crazy Ostrich Run

Crazy Ostrich Run is a prototype 2D endless runner game. The focus on this project was to try and get a feel of the gameplay and mechanics of the game while also trying to keep an open idea of the theme of the game, hence why there is not much in terms of implemented graphics.

So far, I feel quite concrete about the idea of controlling an ostrich that is running through some sort of desert themed environment, hence why I have decided to call it Crazy Ostrich Run. I had some other ideas as well, but I feel from a visual standpoint and my artistic ability, drawing an ostrich and desert themed place seemed to be the most within my capabilities.
