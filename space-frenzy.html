Space Frenzy

Space Frenzy is another game built using my 2D game engine, although it is an unfinished project, I was able to develop concrete concepts that would be the basis of Space Frenzy. As you can see in the videos, the main gameplay of Space Frenzy is controlling a ship in cyber space, going through the levels and defeating or avoiding enemies along the way. One of the other main features of Space Frenzy is that the player would be able to build their ship, which would give their ship different stats/weapons depending on what kind of blocks they used to build their ship.

I am thinking of transitioning the project to Unity, where when the time is right, I will be able to transition it to different platforms such as iOS.
