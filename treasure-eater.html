Treasure Eater

Treasure Eater is a 3D game made in Unity. The aim of the game is to roll around the player (whom I have named Blob) to eat gems that are big enough to fit in Blob's mouth, and avoid getting squished by the bigger gems.

This was a project that challenged many aspects of my creativity, especially the graphical aspect of it as I have produced all the 3D models on blender, and produced the textures either through blender or photoshop.
